Full Weekend Events

Our Big Events are our Weekend Events. These run from Friday evening (usually at 5pm) until Sunday afternoon (usually 3pm) once per month. This allows us to give players some very interesting combats, role-playing events and more that will further our plot lines of our overall story arc as well as any plots that the PCs present to us.


PCs are an immense support for our Plot. We write the beginning of each event with a place we would like it to go. The players, through their actions, inactions and interactions, make up the middle and can (and usually do) directly affect the end of the weekend and set up the events of the next month's weekend.

Tavern Night-Single Day

As most people know, winter weather is normally COLD! So during the "off" months that we cannot put on weekend events (typically November to April) many chapters will try to put on a tavern night or Masquerade night. These will be typically held on a Saturday and will help to keep everyone active during the cold months. These will have smaller "modules" with them and be completely IG with Experience, Refuge Points etc.

Online Events

during colder months, and now with COVID playing havoc with our LARPing, many chapters are running "online" events through their Discord Channels. Please check out the links for each chapter in the above menu and that should lead you to their discord servers. Experience may be handed out for the event, that is still up to the Refuge Board of Directors.
